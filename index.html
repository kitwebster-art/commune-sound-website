Commune blends the energy of a rave with a deeper movement practice.

The music moves through ambient soundscapes, progressive and rhythm-driven acoustic and electronic music, paired with programmed lighting controlled live throughout the night. A place to drop in, soak up the sound and move.

Just a beautiful room and dancefloor stripped back to the essentials.
